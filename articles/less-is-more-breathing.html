How Your Lungs Work ‚Äî The Journey of Air

Step 1: Inhalation. Air travels through your nose, down the trachea, into bronchi and bronchioles, terminating in tiny air sacs called alveoli ‚Äî approximately 300-500 million of them, providing a surface area roughly the size of a tennis court.
