Sri Lalitha Sahasranama

The Thousand Divine Names of the Supreme Goddess

From the Brahmanda Purana · Dialogue between Hayagriva and Agastya

Nyasa न्यासः

Dhyana Shlokas ध्यानम्

Meditation verses to invoke the Divine Mother before chanting

Atha Sri Lalitha Sahasranama Stotram अथ श्रीललितासहस्रनामस्तोत्रम्

Each shloka is shown with its component divine names broken down below

evaM shrIlalitA devyA nAmnAM sAhasrakaM jaguH

Thus the thousand names of Sri Lalita Devi have been sung.

iti shrIbrahmANDapurANe uttarakhaNDe shrIhayagrIvAgastyasaMvAde
shrIlalitA sahasranAma stotra kathanaM sampUrNam

Thus ends the narration of Sri Lalita Sahasranama Stotra from the Uttara Khanda of the Brahmanda Purana, in the dialogue between Sri Hayagriva and Agastya.
