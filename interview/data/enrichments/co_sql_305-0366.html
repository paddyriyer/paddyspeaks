Airbnb's search-to-book funnel is the central conversion metric. The query joins searches to subsequent bookings within 30 days and computes per-property-type conversion rates.

1. The source data — search_events + bookings

2. The SQL — time-bounded LEFT JOIN

SELECT s.property_type,
  COUNT(DISTINCT s.search_id) AS searches,
  COUNT(DISTINCT b.user_id) AS booked_users,
  ROUND(100.0 * COUNT(DISTINCT b.user_id) / NULLIF(COUNT(DISTINCT s.user_id), 0), 1) AS user_book_pct
FROM search_events s
LEFT JOIN bookings b
  ON b.user_id = s.user_id
 AND b.listing_id = s.viewed_listing_id
 AND julianday(b.booked_at) - julianday(s.searched_at) BETWEEN 0 AND 30
GROUP BY s.property_type
ORDER BY user_book_pct DESC;

3. Strategic explanations

The 30-day window — Airbnb's published attribution

Airbnb measures conversion within 30 days of search. Shorter windows (7d) miss vacation-planners; longer (90d) over-attribute. 30 is the operational compromise.

The exact-listing-match strictness

The query requires user to book the SAME listing they searched. Real-world: users browse 10 listings, book 1. Counting only exact matches under-attributes. Loosen by joining on (user_id, property_type, city) for "anything close" attribution.

Searches vs unique-users

The query mixes search_id (impressions) and user_id (unique users). For "user_book_pct" the denominator should be unique users. Be explicit about which counter you're using.

Property-type segmentation

Conversion rates differ structurally:

property_typetypical conversion
Entire home4-8% (high intent)
Private room2-4% (lower intent, multi-search)
Hotel1-3% (compares against Booking.com)
The seasonality / lead-time trap

Searches in November for a March vacation have a 4-month lag — won't book in 30 days. The 30-day window misses these. For long-lead-time bookings, use a 90-day attribution.

Search-to-message vs search-to-book

Some users message hosts (questions about pets, group size) before booking. Search-to-message is a leading indicator that converts to book at 60% rate. Add the intermediate step for early-funnel insight.

Senior signal: pin the 30-day window as published-and-conventional, distinguish exact-match vs property-match attribution, separate impressions from unique users, and surface property-type baseline differences. The search-to-message intermediate-step funnel is the over-the-bar refinement.
